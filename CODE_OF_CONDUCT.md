# Elara Project Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as members, contributors, and leaders of the Elara Project, pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are committed to fostering an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and supportive community that empowers individuals to shape Elara’s story through meaningful interactions and creative contributions.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment for our community include:

* Showing empathy, kindness, and respect toward others, celebrating diverse perspectives in storytelling and technology.
* Engaging respectfully with differing opinions, whether about Elara’s narrative or project direction.
* Offering and accepting constructive feedback to improve the project and community experience.
* Taking responsibility for mistakes, apologizing sincerely, and learning to strengthen our community.
* Prioritizing the collective growth of Elara and her community over individual interests.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Using sexualized language, imagery, or making unwelcome advances.
* Engaging in trolling, insulting comments, or personal, political, or derogatory attacks.
* Harassing others publicly or privately, including on X or other platforms.
* Sharing others’ private information (e.g., physical or email addresses) without explicit consent.
* Spamming issues, pull requests, or X posts with unrelated links or promotions.
* Any conduct reasonably considered inappropriate in a collaborative, professional setting.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Elara Project community leaders are responsible for upholding these standards and will take fair, appropriate action in response to any behavior deemed inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Leaders may remove, edit, or reject contributions (e.g., comments, commits, issues, pull requests) that violate this Code of Conduct, and will provide clear reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all Elara Project community spaces, including GitHub, X, GitBook, and any official communication channels. It also applies when individuals represent the project publicly, such as posting from the official X account (@ProjectElarax), using an official email, or speaking at events.

## Enforcement

Report instances of abusive, harassing, or unacceptable behavior to the community leaders at elara.project.feedback@gmail.com. All complaints will be reviewed promptly, fairly, and confidentially.

Community leaders will respect the privacy and security of the reporter and all parties involved in any incident.

## Enforcement Guidelines

Community leaders will follow these Community Impact Guidelines to determine consequences for violations:

### 1. Correction

**Community Impact**: Use of inappropriate language or behavior deemed unprofessional or unwelcome.

**Consequence**: A private, written warning from community leaders, explaining the violation and why the behavior was inappropriate. A public apology may be requested.

### 2. Warning

**Community Impact**: A single incident or series of actions violating community standards.

**Consequence**: A warning with consequences for continued behavior, including no interaction with involved parties (including leaders) for a specified period. This applies to community spaces and external platforms like X. Violations may lead to a temporary or permanent ban.

### 3. Temporary Ban

**Community Impact**: A serious violation, such as sustained inappropriate behavior.

**Consequence**: A temporary ban from all community interactions (e.g., GitHub, X) for a specified period. No contact with involved parties or leaders is allowed. Violations may lead to a permanent ban.

### 4. Permanent Ban

**Community Impact**: A pattern of violations, including harassment, aggression, or disparagement of individuals or groups.

**Consequence**: A permanent ban from all public community interactions.
